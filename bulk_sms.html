Bulk SMS Service

BulkSMS service allows you to send SMS to a number or group of numbers within minutes. So, you can deliver any information to your desire phone number fast and easily.

Bulk SMS is helpful for all types of businesses like social and business networking group, Information technology companies, wholesale and retail stores, Real estate services, financial services, Human resource services, Travel &Tourism particularly in goal of reaching local customers. We offer affordable Bulk SMS Service for communication and promotion of product and services of your all Business.

Features:
  • Single and Bulk Text Messaging.
  • Send SMS from your Sender ID (Maximum 11 Character).
  • Use your Product / Service or Website name as Sender ID.
  • Supported SMS format are Normal Text SMS, Unicode SMS and Flash SMS.
  • Upload the number list from an excel sheet. CSV Format or Notepad files (Txt.) Format.
  • Developer API (You can combine SMS Solution with your software and can be automated).
  • Grouping Functionality.
  • Single click SMS delivery to thousands of numbers.
  • SMS Scheduling.
  • History log (Instant, Mass SMS, and Scheduled etc.).
  • Easy Web Interface with password protection.
  • Send SMS History.
  • Online Address Book.
  • Real time delivery report and unlimited storage of all reports.
  • MIS Reports.
  • No setup fees, No monthly fees, No hidden costs.
  • 100% Security and Privacy Protection.
  • Fast Delivery to all networks Nationwide.
  • Free Chat/Email/Telephone Support.
